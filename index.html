HOW TO PLAY

  • Objective: Control the monkey and jump over incoming hippos.
  • Controls: Press SPACE, UP ARROW, or CLICK to jump.
  • Scoring: Each hippo dodged gives you 1 point.
  • Speed: Game speed increases every 10 points.
  • Avoid: If the monkey hits a hippo, the game ends.

Tip: Time your jumps carefully - hippos come at different heights!
